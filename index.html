What we offer

Three formats, one practice.

Every engagement starts with a chat. We listen first, then co-design a format calibrated to your team, your challenges and the conversation you're ready to have.

01

Workshops

Facilitated workshops that create space for honest conversations about collaboration, intercultural dynamics, bias, and discrimination. Participants leave with practical tools, shared language and a go-to plan tailored to your needs.

Topics may include

  • Unconscious bias
  • Gender, sexuality and sexism
  • Anti-racism
  • Intercultural dynamics
  • Allyship in practice
  • Inclusive communication
  • Body image and body shaming
  • Cultural appropriation

Personalised format

In-person, online or hybrid

Up to 15 participants

Recommended duration: 3+ hours

About

Two voices, one practice.

We have been close friends for over a decade, united by shared interests: a passion for travel, languages, music, and a genuine curiosity about people and cultures. Having both grown up between different countries, we bring a nuanced perspective on belonging, communication, and diversity.

Let's Talk, Team! emerged from countless in-depth conversations and our professional experience across different sectors. Together, we combine expertise in media with a strong foundation in anti-discrimination work to create open, respectful, and equitable dialogue — always at eye level.
